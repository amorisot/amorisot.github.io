    micro-pmf

    The existence of micromorts and micromarriages implies the existence of micro-pmf's, associated with actions you take to increase the probability that your startup builds something that reaches product-market fit.

    An incomplete (and largely facetious) list:

    - Thinking about your product in the shower = 1 micro-pmf - Talking to a customer = 2 micro-pmfs - Listening to a customer = 10 micro-pmfs - Hiring an engineer = 10 micro-pmfs - Hiring a 10x engineer = 100 micro-pmfs - Hiring a 100x engineer = 1 milli-pmf - Closing a deal = 100 micro-pmfs - Going to a conference to network = -10 micro-pmfs - Having heated debates with your cofounders = 25 micro-pmfs

    I'll update these, probably with more earnesty, as my co-proprietorship of my small business evolves.
