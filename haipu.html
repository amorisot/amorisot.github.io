    There are many miscellaneous tracking apps (for food, runs, poops) in the world, and I've tried using them in the past, but have broadly found the unengaging. Haipu is an attempt at creating a poop tracking app that sparks joy, by treating the user to a generated haiku after each poop. The hope is that the haikus are compelling and amusing enough that people will keep using the app.

    If you need support or have feedback about Haipu, please email me!
